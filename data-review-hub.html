How to Cite

          

            
When referencing data from COHO Analytics in publications or reports, use:

            COHO Analytics. (). Colorado Housing Analytics Platform. Retrieved from https://coho.analytics/data-review-hub.html
            

              Individual dataset citations should also include the original source provider (e.g. FRED, Census Bureau, CHFA) and the lastUpdated date shown in the Sources tab.
