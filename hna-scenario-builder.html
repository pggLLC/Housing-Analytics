Projection Scenario Builder

Create and compare demographic growth scenarios through 2050 using the cohort-component model. Adjust fertility, migration, and mortality assumptions — then save custom scenarios for later comparison.

Screening tool only. Projections are illustrative planning estimates based on public DOLA and Census data. They are not certified forecasts and do not substitute for a formal housing needs study or CHFA-required analysis.

Select Geography

    Counties use DOLA Colorado State Demography Office single-year-of-age population pyramids. Incorporated places and Census-Designated Places (CDPs / unincorporated communities) use the containing county's pyramid, scaled down by the sub-county's share of county population (ACS B01003). The downscaling preserves the age and sex distribution of the surrounding county — appropriate for most Colorado sub-county geographies but may understate the youth/working-age skew in college towns and resort areas. The headship rate (0.38) and target vacancy (5%) apply identically.
