Affordable Housing Intelligence is a data-driven platform delivering market intelligence for developers, investors, housing authorities, and policymakers navigating the increasingly complex landscape of housing affordability, capital markets, and community development. By integrating economic indicators, housing supply metrics, demographic trends, and predictive market data, the platform provides a clear, objective foundation for evaluating development feasibility, financing conditions, and long-term housing need.

Whether underwriting a LIHTC development, evaluating permanent loan sizing, or assessing regional housing demand, sound decisions depend on understanding both present conditions and forward-looking risk.
