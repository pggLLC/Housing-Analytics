COHO Analytics is a data-driven platform delivering market intelligence for developers, housing authorities, and policymakers navigating the complex landscape of housing affordability, community development, and long-term housing need. By integrating economic indicators, housing supply metrics, demographic trends, and public data, the platform provides a clear, objective foundation for evaluating development feasibility, financing conditions, and community housing demand.

Whether underwriting a LIHTC development, evaluating permanent loan sizing, or assessing regional housing demand, sound decisions depend on understanding both present conditions and forward-looking risk. This platform brings together trusted public datasets and real-time market signals to support disciplined analysis.

Built for affordable housing professionals, public agencies, and community development practitioners, COHO Analytics provides the analytical context needed to support durable, financially viable, and policy-aligned housing outcomes.
