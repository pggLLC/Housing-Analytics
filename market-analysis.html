Methodology & Data Sources

ACS data: US Census Bureau American Community Survey 5-Year Estimates (B01003, B25003, B25004, B25070, B25064). LIHTC: HUD LIHTC Database (public). Geographies: Census TIGERweb tract centroids. Enhanced delineation: LEHD/LODES commuting flows, USGS hydrology, school boundaries (ED), NTD transit, EPA Smart Location, HUD NHPD, Opportunity Zones, FEMA flood zones. All data is pre-computed or fetched from free public APIs; no proprietary key required. See MARKET_ANALYSIS_METHOD.md, PMA_SCORING.md, and PMA_DATA_ENHANCEMENTS.md for full methodology.
