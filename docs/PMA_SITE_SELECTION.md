# PMA Site Selection Guide

## PMA Definitions
- **PMA (Project Management Analysis)**: A structured methodology to assess factors impacting project execution.
- **Site Selection**: The process of evaluating potential locations for project implementation based on predefined criteria.

## Scoring Formulas
- **Total Score Calculation**:  
  \( \text{Total Score} = \sum_{i=1}^{n} (Weight_i \times Score_i) \)  
  Where \( Weight_i \) is the importance of criterion \( i \) and \( Score_i \) is the score obtained for that criterion.

## Site Selection Algorithm
1. **Identify Criteria**: Define the criteria for evaluation (e.g., cost, accessibility, environmental impact).
2. **Assign Weights**: Assign weights to each criterion based on importance.
3. **Evaluate Locations**: Score each location based on the criteria.
4. **Calculate Total Scores**: Use the scoring formula to calculate total scores for each location.
5. **Select Optimal Site**: Choose the site with the highest total score.

## Implementation Notes
- Ensure all criteria are quantifiable.
- Involve stakeholders in defining weights and scores to ensure alignment.

## Explainability Guidelines
- Maintain transparency in scoring and weighting decisions.
- Document rationale for scores and weights to aid future audits and validations.
