Affordable Housing Partnership Pipeline

Internal working dashboard for tracking our active partnership conversations with Colorado communities — focused on small-to-mid rural and recreational towns where a developer can be a thoughtful, place-based partner.

How a conversation moves through the pipeline
  • Screen — passed the Opportunity Finder filter; partnership conversation not yet started.
  • Signal — a recent partnership-relevant moment is documented (RFP, joint conversation, public-land discussion, or funding window).
  • Outreach — you're in active conversation with the community.
  • IC — internal review of the opportunity is underway.
  • Active — working together on a defined project.
  • On Pause — moved to the anti-targets list; explicitly set aside to revisit later.

The live pipeline data (kanban, signal log, anti-targets, network) is maintained in a private workspace and is no longer published on this site. The full methodology is documented on the Pipeline page.
