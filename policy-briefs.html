What this page does. Pulls headlines from public RSS feeds about Colorado affordable housing each day. Groups them by topic and writes a short summary using a language model (GPT-4o-mini).

What this page is not. Editorial reporting. Summaries are machine-generated and have not been independently fact-checked. Treat them as context for what's being reported, not as primary sources.

How to use it. Read the summary, then click into the brief to see the underlying headlines. Open the original article before quoting a fact in a deal memo, board meeting, or grant application.
