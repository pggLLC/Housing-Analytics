Historical Trends & Benchmarks

What have past LIHTC deals in Colorado actually looked like?

Three lenses on Colorado's LIHTC history: CHFA award patterns (helps calibrate a QAP submission), LIHTC stock trajectory (shows when each county absorbed its investment), and a peer benchmark table (find comparable projects by county + size).

Historical data, not forecasts. These panels describe what happened — they do not predict future awards, pricing, or market behavior. Use for calibration, not underwriting.

CHFA Annual Award History

How many 9% competitive vs. 4% PAB-backed awards CHFA has made each year, with aggregate scoring statistics. Use this to calibrate your QAP submission \u2014 a 78-point score was the 2015\u20132025 average; median was 80. Colorado awards ~10 projects/year against ~27 applications (37% overall win rate).

Source: chfa-awards-historical.json \u00b7 Verify current QAP cycle at CHFA QAP

LIHTC Stock Trajectory (HUD Database)

Cumulative Colorado LIHTC projects placed-in-service over time (line, left axis) with annual placement counts (bars, right axis). Shows when each wave of state investment landed \u2014 the mid-2010s multifamily expansion, the 2019\u20132021 acceleration, and the lag from allocation to placement.
