Privacy Policy

How COHO Analytics handles data, cookies, and third-party services.

Data Collection: This platform does not collect, store, or sell personal information. We do not require account registration, and no personally identifiable information (PII) is requested or retained by this site.

Analytics & Tracking: This site does not currently use third-party analytics or advertising trackers. If analytics are added in the future, this policy will be updated and users will be notified via a banner.

Cookies: This site uses only functional cookies necessary for features such as dark-mode preference persistence. No advertising or cross-site tracking cookies are set. You may clear cookies at any time through your browser settings without affecting access to site content.

Third-Party Services: Data displayed on this platform is retrieved from public APIs operated by third parties, including the Federal Reserve Bank of St. Louis (FRED), the U.S. Census Bureau, the Bureau of Labor Statistics, HUD, CHFA (Colorado Housing and Finance Authority) via ArcGIS, and Esri/ArcGIS mapping services. When your browser fetches data directly from these services, those providers' own privacy policies apply. This site does not control and is not responsible for their data practices.

Map Tiles: Interactive maps are rendered using Leaflet with basemap tiles that may be served by OpenStreetMap contributors or other tile providers. Tile requests may log your IP address per those providers' standard server logs. See OpenStreetMap Foundation Privacy Policy for details.

External Links: This site links to external government and industry resources. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those external sites. Links are provided for reference only and do not constitute endorsement.

Data Retention: Because no personal data is collected by this site, there is no user data to retain or delete. Cached public datasets (e.g., data/fred-data.json) contain only aggregated economic series with no personal information.

Your Rights: As this site collects no personal data, there is no personal information to access, correct, or delete. If you believe any content on this platform violates your rights or contains inaccurate information about you, please contact us.

Contact: Privacy questions or concerns may be directed to the maintainers via the project repository at github.com/pggLLC/Housing-Analytics.

Policy Updates: This privacy policy may be updated as site features evolve. The most current version is always available on this page.
